What We Do

Umistrong is a group that provides art materials and art classes for all age groups. We aim to help art enthusiasts, artists, art teachers and students to find the most suitable materials. We keep sourcing products at all price range for different customer groups from youngest child to professional.

We also provide many kinds of art classes for different age groups, such as Oil Painting, Acrylic Painting, Watercolor, Chinese ink, sketching and clay&ceramic art, etc. In addition for kids, we also conduct creative handcraft program. Our studio aims to help learners develop their interest and express creative thinking with brushes and colors.
